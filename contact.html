Frequently Asked Questions

These are some frequently asked questions and here are the answers!

  • 1.How does crowdfunding work on ResoBridge?

    Crowdfunding on ResoBridge allows students and staff to raise funds for specific campus projects or needs. To participate:
    1. Students/Staff can create a campaign for resources needed.
    2. Donors can contribute funds to active campaigns via a secure payment gateway.
    3. Once a campaign reaches its goal, the funds are allocated to the specified project (e.g., repairing a building or purchasing equipment).

  • 2.How can I donate to a campaign?

    To donate to a campaign:
    1. Visit the Crowdfunding page.
    2. Select the active campaign you'd like to support
    3. Choose your donation amount or enter a custom amount.
    4. Select your payment method (e.g., credit card, mobile money, or Payaza).
    5. Confirm your donation.

  • 3.How is the money from crowdfunding managed?

    1. The funds raised through campaigns are managed transparently:
    2. Each campaign displays a detailed breakdown of how the funds will be spent.
    3. Once the goal is reached, the funds are used solely for the campaign's stated purpose (e.g., buying materials, paying for labor).
    4. Campaign updates and progress reports will be provided regularly, allowing donors to see how the funds are being used.

  • 4.Can I modify or delete my complaint after submission?

    Currently, complaints cannot be modified or deleted once submitted. However, if there are updates or additional information that needs to be added, you can contact support to have it updated or amended. . How do I reset my password? If you've forgotten your password, follow these steps:
    1. Click on “Forgot Password?” on the login page.
    2. Enter your email address.
    3. Follow the instructions in the email you receive to reset your password.

  • 5.How do I reset my password?

    If you've forgotten your password, follow these steps:
    1. Click on “Forgot Password?” on the login page.
    2. Enter your email address.
    3. Follow the instructions in the email you receive to reset your password.

  • 6.Can I access ResoBridge on my mobile device?

    Yes! ResoBridge is mobile-friendly and can be accessed through any device with a browser. You can easily submit complaints, track progress, and donate to campaigns on your mobile device.

  • 7.What if a campaign doesn't reach its funding goal?

    If a campaign doesn't meet its goal, the following happens:
    1. The campaign can be extended to gather more contributions.
    2. Partial funds may still be allocated if applicable, or a refund process may be initiated as per the terms outlined in the campaign.

  • 8.Can I suggest a new feature for ResoBridge?

    We love feedback! You can submit your feature suggestions through our Feedback form available in your dashboard. We continuously improve ResoBridge based on user needs.
